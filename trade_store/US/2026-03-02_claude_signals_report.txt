## RISK MANAGEMENT SUMMARY

### **Portfolio Construction Guidelines:**

1. **Position Sizing:**
   - **High Conviction (3+ confirmations or 100% win rate + high alpha):** 3-5% per position
   - **Medium Conviction (1-2 confirmations, 80-95% win rate):** 2-3% per position
   - **Lower Conviction (small sample <10 trades or moderate alpha):** 1-2% per position

2. **Stop-Loss Discipline:**
   - **Never exceed 2% portfolio risk per position** (position size × stop distance)

   - **Exit immediately** if stop breached; do not second-guess
